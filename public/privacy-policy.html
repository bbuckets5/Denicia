Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you contact us, purchase tickets, or create an account, we gather and save information. Your personal data is stored in our client databases, which include our ticketing platform for event access management, payment platform for order processing, and customer service platform for handling your inquiries.

How We Use Your Information

In order to execute your order, send you customer service emails with confirmations, and collect payment, we collect and operate on the data you provide when you do business with us.

Who We Share Your Information With

Some of our third-party service providers can be situated outside of your reach. These include:

  • Companies that supply the technological framework that underpins our system;
  • Payment platforms that supply the software needed to handle payments; and
  • Marketing companies, who supply the data analytics, marketing communications, and advertising that we use.

Data Transfer

Systems operating outside of your state, province, nation, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction may receive and retain your information, including personal data.

Please be aware that if you choose to give us information while you are not in The Bahamas, we will transfer and process your data including personal data in The Bahamas. Your agreement to that transfer is shown by your acceptance of this privacy statement and your subsequent input of such data.

Links to Other Websites

Links to external websites that are not run by us may be included in our service. You will be taken to the website of the third party if you click on one of their links.

It is highly recommended that you read the Privacy Policy of any website you visit.

The content, privacy practices, and policies of any third-party websites or services are beyond our control and responsibility.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We are capable of making changes to our privacy policy. Any updates will be communicated to you by posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page.

It is recommended that you routinely examine this policy on privacy for any updates. Any modifications to this privacy policy take effect as soon as it is published on this page.
